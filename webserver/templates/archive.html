About the Archive

Data Retention: The GMDI archive stores historical measurements from all active CMLs. This data is retained for long-term analysis and research purposes.

Data Quality: All records in the archive have passed basic quality control checks. However, some CMLs may have periods of missing or interrupted data due to maintenance or technical issues.

Statistical Aggregation: The statistics shown above represent aggregated data across all archived measurements. For detailed analysis, individual CML records can be extracted through the API.
